Aplikasi AnTuTu diblokir Google di Android

KOMPAS.com - Nasib sial kembali menimpa aplikasi pengujian (benchmark) performa ponsel bikinan perusahaan asal China, AnTuTu. Setelah dihapus dari gudang aplikasi Android, Play Store beberapa bulan lalu, Google kini berupaya untuk menghadang pengguna memasang (install) aplikasi tersebut di ponsel Android mereka. Pantauan KompasTekno, Rabu (8/7/2020), halaman resmi unduhan file APK aplikasi AnTutu tampak diblokir oleh browser buatan Google, yakni Chrome. Baca juga: Ini Sebab Google Hapus Software Benchmark AnTuTu dari Play Store Alasannya karena laman file.antutu.com dianggap mengandung aplikasi berbahaya yang bisa mengumpulkan data pengguna, berikut melacak lokasi mereka.

KOMPAS.com - Misfortune returned to the application testing (benchmark) performance of mobile phones made by companies from China, AnTuTu. After being removed from the Android application warehouse, Play Store a few months ago, Google is now trying to prevent users from installing the application on their Android phones. KompasTekno observations, Wednesday (07/08/2020), the official page for downloading the AnTutu app APK file appears blocked by Google's browser, Chrome. Also read: This Is Because Google Delete AnTuTu Benchmark Software from Play Store The reason is because the file.antutu.com page is considered to contain dangerous applications that can collect user data, as well as tracking their location.


Tak hanya itu, meski file APK-nya berhasil diunduh dari beragam situs penyedia aplikasi pihak ketiga, pemasangan aplikasi itu kembali dihadang oleh Google lewat fitur pelindung ponsel Android dari aplikasi berbahaya (malware), bernama Play Protect.

Not only that, although the APK file was successfully downloaded from various third-party application provider sites, installation of the application was again blocked by Google through the Android phone's protective feature from a malicious application (malware), called Play Protect.

Peringatan yang bakal dilihat pengguna kurang lebih sama dengan apa yang tercantum di situs tadi, yakni AnTuTu dianggap sebagai aplikasi berbahaya. Sebagaimana dihimpun KompasTekno dari AndroidPolice, Rabu (8/7/2020), fitur Play Protect ini bakal memblokir instalasi aplikasi AnTuTu versi versi 8.3.5 ke atas, di mana aplikasi yang kami jajal sendiri sudah berada di versi 8.4.1.

The warning the user will see is more or less the same as what was listed on the site, AnTuTu is considered a dangerous application. As compiled by KompasTekno from AndroidPolice, Wednesday (07/08/2020), the Play Protect feature will block the installation of the AnTuTu version version 8.3.5 and above, where the application that we tested has been in version 8.4.1.

Meski demikian, pengguna bisa saja mengabaikan peringatan Google ini dan tetap melanjutkan proses instalasi AnTuTu di ponsel dengan meng-klik tombol "Install anyway".

However, users can simply ignore this Google warning and continue the installation process of AnTuTu on their mobile by clicking the "Install anyway" button.

Terlepas dari itu, belum diketahui apa alasan Google berbuat demikian. Sebab, baik Google maupun AnTuTu belum memberikan keterangan resmi terkait kabar perubahaan tersebut.

Apart from that, it is not yet known what the reason Google does so. Because, both Google and AnTuTu have not provided official information related to the news of the change.

Yang jelas, kabar ini agaknya membuktikan bahwa Google seakan ingin meyakinkan pengguna agar tidak memasang aplikasi tersebut, menyusul penghapusan aplikasi AnTuTu dari Play Store sekitar Maret lalu.

What is clear, this news seems to prove that Google seemed to want to convince users not to install the application, following the removal of the AnTuTu application from the Play Store around last March.

Kala itu, pihak AnTuTu mengonfirmasi bahwa alasan penghapusan aplikasinya dari Play Store dilatarbelakangi oleh adanya keterkaitan antara AnTuTu dengan pengembang aplikasi yang dianggap nakal oleh Google, yakni Cheetah Mobile (CM).

At that time, AnTuTu confirmed that the reason for deleting the application from the Play Store was motivated by the association between AnTuTu and application developers deemed naughty by Google, namely Cheetah Mobile (CM).

CM sendiri diketahui kerap melakukan pelanggaran atas kebijakan Google, seperti mengubah aplikasi browser default dari Chrome ke browser buatan mereka sendiri.

CM itself is known to frequently violate Google's policies, such as changing the default browser application from Chrome to their own browser.

Sebagai tindakan tegas, perusaahan asal Mountain View, California, AS, itu mencabut semua aplikasi buatan Cheetah Mobile, dari toko aplikasi Play Store pada Februari lalu.

As a firm measure, the company from Mountain View, California, USA, revoked all applications made by Cheetah Mobile, from the Play Store application store in February.

Hingga saat ini, aplikasi AnTuTu sendiri masih hilang dari Play Store. Namun, aplikasi benchmarking ini bisa ditemukan dan diunduh dengan normal lewat toko aplikasi iOS, App Store.

Until now, the AnTuTu application itself is still missing from the Play Store. However, this benchmarking application can be found and downloaded normally through the iOS application store, App Store.
